Trip Itinerary: Richmond (BC) to Nanaimo (BC)

Dates: July 5th - July 7th, 2025

Hotel: Coast Bastion Hotel, Nanaimo

Day 1: July 5th - Departure from Richmond to Nanaimo
Time Activity
7:45 AM Leave from Home
Address: 9399 Alexandra Road, Richmond, V6X 0L7. Head to Tsawwassen Ferry Terminal.
8:00 AM - 9:30 AM Arrive at Tsawwassen Ferry Terminal
Check-in for the 10:15 AM sailing.
10:15 AM - 12:15 PM Ferry ride from Tsawwassen to Duke Point (Nanaimo)
Relax and enjoy the scenic ferry ride.
12:30 PM - 1:00 PM Arrival at Nanaimo & Drive to Hotel
Coast Bastion Hotel (15 min drive).
1:30 PM - 4:00 PM Explore Downtown Nanaimo
Walk to Harbourfront Walkway, visit Newcastle Island (optional).
4:00 PM - 6:00 PM Lunch & Explore Local Spots
Lunch at Minnoz Restaurant or nearby (The Nest Bistro, Longwood Brew Pub).
6:30 PM Dinner and Relax
Dining options: Harbour City Pub, Ristorante Il Pappalino. Explore Old City Quarter for shopping.
8:00 PM Return to Hotel
Relax and enjoy the view from your room.
Day 2: July 6th - Full Day in Nanaimo
Time Activity
8:00 AM - 9:00 AM Breakfast at Minnoz Lounge (Optional)
Not Free: Cooked-to-order breakfast.
9:00 AM - 12:00 PM Visit Local Attractions
Nanaimo Museum, Petroglyph Park, E & N Rail Trail.
12:30 PM - 2:00 PM Lunch at The Vault Café or Moxie’s Grill
Casual lunch options with great ambiance.
2:00 PM - 5:00 PM More Local Exploration
Visit Piper’s Lagoon Park, Butterfly Garden.
5:30 PM Dinner at The Grand Hotel Restaurant
Casual fine dining.
8:00 PM Evening Walk along the Harbour
Relax and stroll along the Harbourfront Walkway.
Day 3: July 7th - Last Day in Nanaimo
Time Activity
8:00 AM - 9:00 AM Breakfast (Optional)
Minnoz Lounge for breakfast (if not already included).
9:00 AM - 12:00 PM Last-Minute Shopping & Exploring
Visit Vancouver Island Regional Library, Nanaimo North Town Centre.
12:00 PM - 1:30 PM Lunch at Bubba’s Big Bites
Casual spot for a last meal in town.
1:30 PM Check-out of Coast Bastion Hotel
Leave for Duke Point Ferry Terminal.
3:15 PM - 5:15 PM Ferry Ride from Nanaimo to Tsawwassen
Take the 3:15 PM ferry back to Tsawwassen.
5:30 PM Arrive in Tsawwassen
Drive back to Richmond, BC. Estimated arrival: 6:30 PM.

Additional Information:

  • Hotel: Coast Bastion Hotel
  • Parking: Secure underground parking ($9/day).
  • Ferry Info: Tsawwassen to Duke Point (Round-trip), Ferry Schedule: BC Ferries
