Vancouver Island Trip Itinerary

Trip from Richmond, BC to Nanaimo (with a day trip to Parksville or Qualicum Beach), staying at the Coast Bastion Hotel from July 5th to July 7th, 2025.

Day 1: July 5th – Richmond to Nanaimo

TimeActivity
7:45 AMLeave from 9399 Alexandra Road, Richmond, BC.
9:00 AM – 10:00 AMArrive at Tsawwassen Ferry Terminal and prepare for ferry departure.
10:15 AM – 12:15 PMTake BC Ferries to Duke Point, Nanaimo.
12:15 PM – 1:00 PMDrive to Coast Bastion Hotel (~10 min) and check in.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PMLunch at Gabriel’s Café.
2:00 PM – 3:30 PMWalk along Harbourfront Walkway, visit Maffeo Sutton Park, then take the ferry to Newcastle Island. Book here.
3:30 PM – 5:00 PMExplore Newcastle Island and relax.
5:00 PM – 5:30 PMReturn to Nanaimo by ferry.
5:30 PM – 6:50 PMReturn to hotel, then drive to Galaxy Cinema and eat at Montana’s.
6:50 PM – 9:30 PMWatch Jurassic Park: Rebirth.
9:30 PMReturn to hotel and rest.

Day 2: July 6th – Day Trip to Parksville / Qualicum Beach

TimeActivity
8:00 AM – 9:00 AMBreakfast at Minnoz Lounge.
9:00 AM – 10:30 AMDrive to Englishman River Falls, explore.
10:30 AM – 11:30 AMVisit Kathok Meditation Center.
11:30 AM – 1:00 PMLunch at Sushi Wara or Sushi Hama in Qualicum Beach.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PMWalk the Heritage Forest.
2:00 PM – 2:30 PMVisit Little Mountain Lookout.
2:30 PM – 3:30 PMExplore Rathtrevor Provincial Park.
3:30 PM – 4:00 PMDrive back to Nanaimo.
4:00 PM – 5:30 PMRelax at the hotel.
5:30 PM – 7:00 PMDinner at Thai Cook.
7:00 PM – 9:00 PMStroll along Nanaimo waterfront.
9:00 PMReturn to hotel.

Day 3: July 7th – Nanaimo Morning & Return

TimeActivity
8:00 AM – 9:00 AMBreakfast at Minnoz Lounge.
9:00 AM – 11:00 AMExplore Newcastle Island, Maffeo Sutton Park, or Nanaimo Museum.
11:30 AM – 12:30 PMLunch at Gabriel’s Café or The Nest Bistro.
1:00 PMDrive to Duke Point Ferry Terminal.
3:15 PM – 5:15 PMFerry to Tsawwassen.
5:30 PMReturn home to Richmond.
