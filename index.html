Vancouver Island Trip Itinerary

Trip from Richmond, BC to Nanaimo with a day trip to Parksville or Qualicum Beach, staying at the Coast Bastion Hotel from July 5th to July 7th, 2025.

Day 1: July 5th – Richmond to Nanaimo

Time Activity
7:45 AMLeave from 9399 Alexandra Road, Richmond, BC.
9:00 AM – 10:00 AMArrive at Tsawwassen Ferry Terminal and prepare for ferry departure.
10:15 AM – 12:15 PMTake BC Ferries from Tsawwassen to Duke Point, Nanaimo.
12:15 PM – 1:00 PMArrive in Nanaimo, drive to Coast Bastion Hotel (~10 minutes), check in.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PMLunch at Gabriel’s Café.
2:00 PM – 3:30 PMExplore Nanaimo: Harbourfront Walkway, Maffeo Sutton Park, ferry to Newcastle Island.
3:30 PM – 5:00 PMExplore Newcastle Island, enjoy café and nature.
5:00 PM – 5:30 PMReturn to Nanaimo by ferry.
5:30 PM – 6:50 PMReturn to hotel, drive to Galaxy Cinema Nanaimo and dine at Montana's.
6:50 PM – 9:30 PMWatch Jurassic Park: Rebirth.
9:30 PMDrive back to Coast Bastion Hotel and rest.

Day 2: July 6th – Parksville or Qualicum Beach Day Trip

Time Activity
8:00 AM – 9:00 AMBreakfast at Minnoz Lounge (at Coast Bastion Hotel).
9:00 AM – 10:30 AMDrive to Englishman River Falls (~42 minutes), explore the area.
10:30 AM – 11:30 AMVisit Kathok Meditation Center (~15 minutes away) for exploration and bai bai.
11:30 AM – 1:00 PMHead to Qualicum Beach, lunch at Sushi Wara or Sushi Hama.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PMExplore Heritage Forest.
2:00 PM – 2:30 PMVisit Little Mountain Lookout.
2:30 PM – 3:30 PMExplore Rathtrevor Provincial Park.
3:30 PM – 4:00 PMDrive back to Nanaimo.
4:00 PM – 5:30 PMRelax at the hotel.
5:30 PM – 7:00 PMDinner at Thai Cook.
7:00 PM – 9:00 PMEvening stroll along Nanaimo’s waterfront.
9:00 PMReturn to Coast Bastion Hotel.

Day 3: July 7th – Morning & Return

Time Activity
8:00 AM – 9:00 AMBreakfast at Minnoz Lounge or nearby café.
9:00 AM – 11:00 AMExplore Nanaimo: boat trip to Newcastle Island, visit Maffeo Sutton Park, or Nanaimo Museum.
11:30 AM – 12:30 PMLunch at Gabriel’s Café or The Nest Bistro.
1:00 PMDrive to Nanaimo Ferry Terminal.
3:15 PM – 5:15 PMTake BC Ferries from Nanaimo to Tsawwassen.
5:30 PMArrive Tsawwassen, drive back to Richmond.
