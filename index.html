Vancouver Island Trip Itinerary

Trip from Richmond, BC to Nanaimo (with a day trip to Parksville or Qualicum Beach), staying at the Coast Bastion Hotel from July 5th to July 7th, 2025.

Day 1: July 5th – Departure from Richmond to Nanaimo

Time Activity
7:45 AM Leave from 9399 Alexandra Road, Richmond, BC.
9:00 AM – 10:00 AM Arrive at Tsawwassen Ferry Terminal and prepare for ferry departure.
10:15 AM – 12:15 PM Take BC Ferries from Tsawwassen to Duke Point, Nanaimo.
12:15 PM – 1:00 PM Arrive in Nanaimo, drive to Coast Bastion Hotel (~10 minutes), and check in.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PM Lunch at Gabriel’s Café.
2:00 PM – 3:30 PM Explore Nanaimo: Walk along the Harbourfront Walkway, visit Maffeo Sutton Park, then take the ferry to Newcastle Island. Book here.
3:30 PM – 5:00 PM Explore Newcastle Island, grab coffee at a café, relax, and enjoy the area.
5:00 PM – 5:30 PM Return to Nanaimo by ferry.
5:30 PM – 6:50 PM Return to hotel, then drive to Galaxy Cinema Nanaimo and dine at Montana's.
6:50 PM – 9:30 PM Watch Jurassic Park: Rebirth at Galaxy Cinema.
9:30 PM Drive back to Coast Bastion Hotel and rest.

Day 2: July 6th – Day Trip to Parksville or Qualicum Beach

Time Activity
8:00 AM – 9:00 AM Breakfast at Minnoz Lounge (inside Coast Bastion Hotel).
9:00 AM – 10:30 AM Drive to Englishman River Falls (~42 minutes) and explore the area.
10:30 AM – 11:30 AM Visit Kathok Meditation Center (~15 minutes away) to explore and do bai bai.
11:30 AM – 1:00 PM Head to Qualicum Beach and have lunch at Sushi Wara or Sushi Hama.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PM Explore the Heritage Forest.
2:00 PM – 2:30 PM Visit Little Mountain Lookout.
2:30 PM – 3:30 PM Explore Rathtrevor Provincial Park.
3:30 PM – 4:00 PM Drive back to Nanaimo.
4:00 PM – 5:30 PM Relax at the hotel.
5:30 PM – 7:00 PM Dinner at a local restaurant: Thai Cook.
7:00 PM – 9:00 PM Evening stroll along Nanaimo’s waterfront.
9:00 PM Return to Coast Bastion Hotel for the night.

Day 3: July 7th – Morning Exploration & Return to Richmond

Time Activity
8:00 AM – 9:00 AM Breakfast at Minnoz Lounge (or nearby café).
9:00 AM – 11:00 AM Explore Nanaimo: Take a boat trip to Newcastle Island, visit Maffeo Sutton Park, or check out the Nanaimo Museum.
11:30 AM – 12:30 PM Lunch in Nanaimo at Gabriel’s Café or The Nest Bistro.
1:00 PM Drive to the Nanaimo Ferry Terminal.
3:15 PM – 5:15 PM Take the BC Ferries from Nanaimo to Tsawwassen.
5:30 PM Arrive at Tsawwassen and head back to Richmond.
