Vancouver Island Trip Itinerary

Trip from Richmond, BC to Nanaimo (with a day trip to Parksville or Qualicum Beach), staying at the Coast Bastion Hotel from July 5th to July 7th, 2025.

Day 1: July 5th – Departure from Richmond to Nanaimo

Time Activity
7:45 AM Leave from 9399 Alexandra Road, Richmond, BC
9:00 AM – 10:00 AM Arrive at Tsawwassen Ferry Terminal and prepare for ferry departure.
10:15 AM – 12:15 PM Take the BC Ferries from Tsawwassen to Duke Point, Nanaimo.
12:15 PM – 1:00 PM Arrive in Nanaimo, drive to Coast Bastion Hotel (~10 minutes), and check-in.
1:00 PM – 2:00 PM Lunch at Gabriel’s Café.
2:00 PM – 3:30 PM Explore Nanaimo: Walk around the Harbourfront Walkway, visit Maffeo Sutton Park, Head to ferry to NewCastle Island Book
3:30 PM – 5:30 PM explore the area of NewCastle Island, go cafe, etc.
5:30 PM - 6:00 PM Head back to Nanaimos by ferry (need to check if ferry is round trip).
6:00 PM – 7:30 PM Dinner at The Lighthouse Bistro (waterfront dining with fresh seafood) or SukkhoThai Restaurant (5pm - 8pm).
7:30 PM – 9:00 PM Explore local breweries: Visit Longwood Brewery or White Sails Brewing or explore downtown.
9:00 PM Return to Coast Bastion Hotel for the night.

Day 2: July 6th – Day Trip to Parksville or Qualicum Beach

Time Activity
8:00 AM – 9:00 AM Breakfast at Minnoz Lounge (at Coast Bastion Hotel).
9:00 AM – 12:00 PM Drive to either Parksville (~40 minutes) to visit Rathtrevor Beach or check out Englishman River Falls, or go to Qualicum Beach (~45 minutes) to stroll the beach and visit the Heritage Forest.
12:00 PM – 1:00 PM Lunch in Parksville or Qualicum Beach.
1:00 PM – 3:00 PM Explore more of the area: Enjoy the beach, visit local parks, or explore the town.
3:00 PM – 4:00 PM Drive back to Nanaimo.
4:00 PM – 5:00 PM Relax back at the hotel or visit more Nanaimo attractions.
5:30 PM – 7:00 PM Dinner at The Nest Bistro or local restaurant in the Old City Quarter.
7:00 PM – 9:00 PM Evening stroll along Nanaimo’s waterfront or visit Newcastle Island by taking a short boat trip.
9:00 PM Return to Coast Bastion Hotel for the night.

Day 3: July 7th – Morning Exploration & Return to Richmond

Time Activity
8:00 AM – 9:00 AM Breakfast at Minnoz Lounge (or nearby café).
9:00 AM – 11:00 AM Explore Nanaimo: Take a boat trip to Newcastle Island, visit Maffeo Sutton Park, or check out the Nanaimo Museum.
11:30 AM – 12:30 PM Lunch in Nanaimo at Gabriel’s Café or The Nest Bistro.
1:00 PM Drive to the Nanaimo Ferry Terminal.
3:15 PM – 5:15 PM Take the BC Ferries from Nanaimo to Tsawwassen.
5:30 PM Arrive at Tsawwassen and head back to Richmond.
